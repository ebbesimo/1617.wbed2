Battlefield I Project

Battlefield 1 is a first-person shooter video game developed by EA DICE and published by Electronic Arts. It's the 15th entry in the Battlefield series with the first one being Battlefield 1942 which was released in 2002. Battlefield 1 was released in October 2016 and was the first big name in the series since Battlefield 4 (October 2013) and Battlefield Hardline (March 2015). The detailed artwork and the realistic artstyle was a fun and challenging feature to take into account whilst working on this project.
