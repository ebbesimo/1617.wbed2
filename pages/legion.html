World of Warcraft: Legion Project

World of Warcraft is a massively multiplayer role-playing game, released in 2004 by Blizzard Entertainment. Legion, launched in August 2016, was the sixth expansion set of the game with its predecessors being Burning Crusade (January 2007) , Wrath of the Lich King (November 2008) , Cataclysm (December 2010) , Mists of Pandaria (September 2012) and Warlords of Draenor (November 2014) . Blizzard Entertainment is a very known company in the world of game development and it was our second collaboration with them.
