How do we work?

A graphic agency has to be unique and creative in its own way. And our inspiration comes from the information that was given by the client. Here at We-All, we try to get as close to the clients' needs as we can. So before we start with a project, we make sure that a collaboration with our agency is beneficial for both our client and our agency. Here a few short steps, describing our working process.

Step 1

The first step is made by the client. They have to contact us to propose the start of a collaboration. There are a few ways to contact us, but the most reliable way of getting in touch with us is by sending us an E-mail. Our mailbox is getting checked multiple times a day to make sure we respond in the shortest time possible. If you want to start a collaboration, make sure it is clarified in the subject so we can immediately see what the intention is of that message.

Step 2

Once the client contacted us, we'll start to dig deeper and inform ourselves more about them. When we think that a collaboration could be in both our interests, we'll send a response in which we clarify that we are interested and would like to have a meeting. This meeting can happen in real life, but can also be arranged over the internet. The goal of this meeting is to learn more about the project, specifically what it intales. If we come to an agreement, we'll go straight to work. With the information given by our client, we start working on ideas and sketch them out. This should take a couple of days.

Step 3

Once the ideas are sketched out we recontact our client. They let us know which ones they like the most and those are the ones we'll use to develop. After a few days, the final results should be ready and they will be send to the client for their approval. If they still have some suggestions, we can easily implement those in the final product. Only when both our client and us are happy about the results, the project is finished.
