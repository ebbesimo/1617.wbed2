Who are we?

W-All is a Belgian Graphic Design company, which we started in September 2015. It's being run by three fellow students who learned to know eachother three years ago. Our goal is to create artwork that is truly unique and accessible. Since we focus ourselves on the world of gaming, every project has it's own unique design and it's own character. We started this company purely with ambition. And thanks to hard work, perseverance and great opportunities, we've already gotten the chance to work for some of the greatest game developers in the world. We are young, full of energy and we're looking forward to the future, whatever it might bring.
